# Password Reset Policy

## Self-service portal

Visit https://password.megacorpone.local and follow the prompts. You
will need your employee ID and email address on file.

## Requirements

- Minimum 12 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character

Passwords expire every 90 days.

## If locked out

Contact the helpdesk at extension 4357 or helpdesk@megacorpone.local.

## Service-account passwords

Service account passwords are rotated quarterly by the IT Operations
team. End users should never set or change a service account password
themselves — open a JIRA ticket against the IT-OPS project.
