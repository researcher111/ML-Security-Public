# Engineering Onboarding

Welcome to MegaCorpAI Engineering. Your first week:

## Day 1
- Pick up your laptop from IT (Floor 4, room 401). The asset tag is your username.
- Sign the Acceptable Use Policy and the IP Assignment Agreement.
- Join the `#new-hires` Slack channel.

## Day 2-3
- Provision your GitHub access via the IT portal under "Source Code".
- Set up your VPN per the Wi-Fi and VPN Troubleshooting guide.
- Schedule a 1:1 with your manager.

## Day 4-5
- Pair-program with your onboarding buddy through the company's "Hello World" exercise.
- Submit your first PR against the `playground` repo.

If anything blocks you, the helpdesk extension is 4357.
